### Phase 2: Pilot Test (Day 2-3)

1. **Select 2-3 Tech-Savvy Drivers**
   - Choose drivers who are comfortable with phones
   - Ideally ones who can give feedback

2. **Share Installation Message** (see below)

3. **Follow Up**
   - Call them after 1 hour
   - Ask: "Did it install ok?"
   - Collect feedback

4. **Monitor Their Entries**
   - Check admin dashboard
   - Ensure entries are coming through
   - Verify data accuracy

---

### Phase 3: Full Rollout (Day 4+)

1. **Prepare WhatsApp Message**
   - Use template below
   - Include your URL
   - Add support contact

2. **Send to All Drivers**
   - Share in driver WhatsApp group
   - Or send individually

3. **Support Period**
   - Be available for calls
   - Answer installation questions
   - Help with any issues

4. **Monitor Dashboard**
   - Watch for entries coming in
   - Identify drivers who haven't installed
   - Follow up personally
